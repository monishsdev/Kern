## What is Kern?

Kern is an operating system built from the ground up for smartwatches — the way Linux transformed personal computing, Kern does the same for the wrist. It's open, hackable, and designed to be genuinely beautiful.

Most smartwatch OSes lock you in. Kern locks nothing. You get a full Linux-based kernel, a live UI builder, a cloud app store, and 15 GB of personal cloud storage — all on your wrist.

---

## Features

### 🎨 UI Builder
Design your own watch face from scratch using a drag-and-drop composer or raw code. Export themes, share them with the community, or keep them private. Every pixel is yours to control.

### 📦 App Store
Install any Kern-compatible app wirelessly from the cloud. Browse 400+ apps across fitness, health, productivity, media, and utility. Sideloading is fully supported from day one.

### ☁️ Cloud Storage — 15 GB per watch
Every Kern watch comes with 15 GB of dedicated personal cloud storage. Sync apps, themes, health data, and files instantly across all your devices. No subscription. No expiry. Your data, your keys.

### 🔧 Open Kernel
Kern runs a full Linux-based kernel. SSH in directly, write custom scripts, automate workflows, and build exactly what you need. Full developer access, always.

### 📱 Phone Companion
Manage, design, and configure your watch from the Kern companion app on iOS and Android. Change themes, install apps, and monitor cloud storage — all from your phone or directly on the watch itself.

### 🔒 Privacy First
All data is encrypted end-to-end. Zero telemetry by default. Fully auditable open source code. Your cloud, your encryption keys, your rules.

## Tech Stack

**Prototype**
- Vanilla HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

**Planned OS Stack**
- Linux kernel (custom ARM / RISC-V build)
- Kern Shell — lightweight Wayland compositor for round/square displays
- Kern Runtime — sandboxed app execution environment
- Kern Cloud — end-to-end encrypted sync infrastructure
